Tembea Africa

Akagera National Park is home for more than 100 bird species, Zebras and Girafes.In 2017 it officially became a "Big Five" park, meaning it is has; Lions, Rhinos, Leopards, Elephants and Buffaloes. If you are outdoorsy and you want to enjoy the park's activities you are in for a treat!There are numerous fun and adventurous activities like: fishing, night drives in the park, boat trips and so much more for you to enjoy.
